Who are we?

We are a group of current and former school administrators who understand the challenges school districts face that work side-by-side with a highly advanced team of computer and artificial intelligence experts. Together we have created software that producesd highly efficient bus routes.

The Process

First, we obtain the locations of the bus stops and school start and end times. Next, the school district provides the non-negotiables that need to be included in its bus routes. Then, our software produces highly efficient bus routes which school districts review and adjust as they see fit.

Business Solution

We use cutting-edge machine learning techniques to produce highly efficient bus routes in hours as opposed to months. Artificial intelligence is also incredibly helpful in quickly trying out various transportation and school start time scenarios to see its impact on the efficiencies and costs of bus routes.

Why AI?

Did you know that trying to compute all the possible ways to stop at 60 locations is equal to all the atoms in the observable universe. Determining bus routes is incredibly complex task to do for Transportation Supervisors. With AI we can produce highly efficient bus routes in hours as opposed to months.

Our Services

The average quantifiable cost savings from reducing one bus is over $65,000 for school districts who worked with us in the previous fiscal year. Our optimized bus routs provide better usage of parking lots, driveways and curbside pickup/dropoff areas at schools, and reduce strain on traffic. 52% of school districts say bus driver shortage was their number one concern. Our services help make school districts less vulnerable to labor shortages by cutting unneeded fleets.
